About Me

Hello! I'm Kylee, the creative mind behind this meal prep side gig. What started as a personal passion for cooking evolved into a fun and practical way to help others enjoy healthy meals on a busy schedule. I experiment with easy, nutritious recipes and share them here to inspire anyone looking for simple meal solutions. Join me on this journey as I balance my full-time work with my love for cooking and healthy living.
